Oh Brendan, the Land of Freedom

Here he is. Brendan, the host of the beautiful land, the god of all his creatures. Keep clam and wait for him. Grass shall grow, sparrows shall tweet, and the princess shall come and meet. Welcome to the Land of Freedom.

Learn More

 

Oh Brendan, also known as the Land of Freedom, is a place founded in 2013. It is named in honour of the host, the Greater Brendan. The earth, which is becoming more and more crowded, is the foundation of Oh Brendan. Thanks to the ancient and mysterious technology, Oh Brendan could be created in a place, which only creatures full of hope can find. And that is also the reason why it is called the Land of Freedom.
